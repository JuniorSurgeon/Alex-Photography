About

Hi! I'm Alex, head (and only) photographer at Alex Lynn Bunker Photography.

My passion for photography began when my daughter, Jane, was born. Soon I realized that time was going much too quickly, and that there was nothing I could do to slow it down.

However, as I began learning how to take beautiful photos of Jane, I realized that I could, in a way, preserve some of our precious moments forever in pictures.

My goal is for everyone to have their beautiful and precious moments preserved forever through beautiful photos. Contact me for family, couple, and individual photo sessions.

I work mostly in Utah Valley and Salt Lake Valleys. I also travel to the Denver area frequently and will update on travel dates to Colorado as soon as they are finalized.
